## GAME THEORY RANKING FRAMEWORK

**Scoring Dimensions** (Pairwise Comparison):

1. **Skills Match** (0-100): How well do her skills align?
2. **Growth Potential** (0-100): Room to advance?
3. **Compensation** (0-100): Meets target ($110K-$160K)?
4. **Culture Fit** (0-100): Mission-driven, values alignment?
5. **Commute** (0-100): SF/Oakland/Marin preferred
6. **Timing** (0-100): When does role start? (Summer 2026 ideal)

**Elo-Style Ranking**:
- Start all roles at 1500 rating
- Pit Role A vs Role B across 6 dimensions
- Winner gains points, loser loses points
- After 100+ comparisons, top-rated roles emerge

**Output**: Ranked list with confidence scores

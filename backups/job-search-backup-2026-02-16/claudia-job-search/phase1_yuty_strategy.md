## SEARCH EXPANSION STRATEGY

**Round 1: Obvious Keywords** (Baseline)
- Search Clusters 1-2
- Expected: 10-20 roles
- Goal: Establish baseline fit criteria

**Round 2: Adjacent Keywords** (Pivot)
- Search Clusters 3-5
- Expected: 30-50 roles
- Goal: Discover roles she wouldn't search for

**Round 3: Unexpected Keywords** (Discovery)
- Search Clusters 6-7
- Expected: 20-40 roles
- Goal: Find "hidden gem" roles

**Round 4: Gradient Descent** (Optimize)
- Analyze which keywords yielded highest fit
- Generate new keyword combinations
- Search again with optimized terms

## DISCOVERY CRITERIA (What Makes a "Hidden Gem")

**Must Have**:
1. Family-facing OR community-building component
2. Cross-functional coordination (not siloed)
3. Systems/process improvement opportunity
4. Mission-driven organization

**Bonus Points**:
5. International/multilingual valued
6. IB or progressive education context
7. Growth/expansion phase (not maintenance)
8. Hybrid role (not pure admissions, not pure ops)

**Red Flags**:
- Heavy fundraising (>50% of role)
- Pure finance/accounting
- 10+ direct reports required
- No family/community touchpoint

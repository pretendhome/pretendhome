Key Framework Extracted:

**5 Essential Assets:**
1. Dividend Growth Stocks (income engine)
2. S&P 500 Index Fund (long-term growth)
3. Income-Producing Real Estate/REITs (inflation hedge)
4. TIPS/I-Bonds (inflation protection)
5. Paid-Off Home (eliminate largest expense)

**Key Principles:**
- Retirement can last 25-30 years (need growth, not just safety)
- Dividends > selling shares (preserve principal)
- Diversification protects against different risks
- Time is the most valuable resource (start early)
- Housing costs are the biggest retirement expense

**Common Mistakes:**
1. Too much in bonds (no growth)
2. Not enough income-producing assets
3. Retiring with a mortgage
4. Lack of diversification
5. Starting too late

**Allocation Guidance by Age:**
- 30s: Focus on index fund + dividend stocks
- 40s: Add real estate, accelerate mortgage paydown
- 50s: Build all 5 assets, pay off home by 60
- 60s: Rebalance for income, ensure housing paid off

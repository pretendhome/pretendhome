# Product Context

**Client**: Rossi Mission  
**Product**: Graffiti Artist Marketplace & Social Enterprise  
**Purpose**: Connect emerging graffiti artists with commercial opportunities while preserving artistic integrity

## Users
- Emerging graffiti artists (primary)
- Commercial buyers (brands, property owners, event organizers)
- Art collectors and enthusiasts
- Community partners and nonprofits

## Goals
- Create sustainable income streams for graffiti artists
- Build marketplace connecting artists with commercial opportunities
- Establish social enterprise model (nonprofit + for-profit hybrid)
- Validate business model with trailing actuals and pipeline data

## Constraints
- Must demonstrate trailing 12-month actuals for underwriting
- Monthly cashflow projections required
- Pipeline validation needed (artist roster + buyer commitments)
- Governance structure must be simplified and defensible
- Bear case scenario must show viability

## Non-goals
- Not a traditional art gallery
- Not a pure nonprofit (hybrid model)
- Not focused on established artists (emerging talent only)

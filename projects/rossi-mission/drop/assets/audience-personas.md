# Audience Persona Cards
**Source**: Argy Research (Phase 1) | **Date**: 2026-02-11

---

## Persona 1: "Writer" — Graffiti Community

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Name** | "Writer" |
| **Age** | 25-55 |
| **Description** | Active or retired graffiti writer. Knows MQ. Knows Tie One. Respects the culture. |
| **Motivation** | Respect for fallen writers. Community solidarity. Authentic collectible. |
| **Budget** | $10-$50 typical, stretches to $200 for something real |
| **Purchase** | Sticker packs, pins, zines, maybe a tee |
| **Channel** | Instagram, 12ozProphet, crew networks, word of mouth |
| **Trigger** | "MQ is doing a real tribute. First time in a gallery." |
| **Turn-off** | Corporate feel, over-priced, explained to them like they don't know |
| **Estimated share** | 40-50% of buyers, 15-20% of revenue |

---

## Persona 2: "Collector" — Urban Art Collector

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Name** | "Collector" |
| **Age** | 30-60 |
| **Description** | Buys contemporary/street art. Follows Artsy, visits galleries, reads Juxtapoz. |
| **Motivation** | Investment potential. Cultural capital. Discovering undervalued artists. Narrative value. |
| **Budget** | $200-$3,000 per piece |
| **Purchase** | Limited prints, originals, hero piece |
| **Channel** | Art press, Instagram, gallery email, Artsy |
| **Trigger** | "1980s NYC graffiti legend, gallery-represented, priced under $3K for originals" |
| **Turn-off** | Unverifiable claims, overpricing beyond market, poor presentation |
| **Estimated share** | 5-10% of buyers, 30-40% of revenue |

---

## Persona 3: "Hype" — Streetwear Consumer

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Name** | "Hype" |
| **Age** | 18-30 |
| **Description** | Follows drops, checks StockX, buys limited edition. Values scarcity and cultural credibility. |
| **Motivation** | FOMO. Social media flex. Resale potential. Authentic street culture (not a corporate collab). |
| **Budget** | $40-$120 |
| **Purchase** | Graphic tees, hoodie, hat, maybe a print |
| **Channel** | Instagram, TikTok, Hypebeast, StockX/Grailed |
| **Trigger** | "Limited drop. One gallery. Two weeks. Then it's done." |
| **Turn-off** | Overproduction, easy availability, no social currency |
| **Estimated share** | 15-25% of buyers, 25-30% of revenue |

---

## Persona 4: "Story" — General Public / Social Justice

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Name** | "Story" |
| **Age** | 25-50 |
| **Description** | Drawn by the narrative. May not know graffiti culture. Cares about justice, Asian-American issues, or SF history. |
| **Motivation** | Emotional connection. Social justice support. Meaningful gift. Cultural preservation. |
| **Budget** | $10-$60 |
| **Purchase** | Zine, sticker pack, tee, open print |
| **Channel** | Local press (SFGate), Asian-American media, podcasts, social media shares |
| **Trigger** | "18-year-old killed for painting. Shooter walked free. 25 years later, one artist still honors him." |
| **Turn-off** | Exploitation of tragedy, commercializing death, insensitivity |
| **Estimated share** | 15-25% of buyers, 10-15% of revenue |

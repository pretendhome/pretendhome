## GAME THEORY: APPROACH 1 VS APPROACH 2

### When Traditional Consulting Wins

**Scenario 1: Time-constrained decision**
- Need recommendations in 1-2 hours
- Human has business expertise to validate
- One-shot analysis, no iteration needed

**Scenario 2: External validation required**
- Need market research (Form 990s, industry reports)
- Comparables outside existing knowledge base
- Credibility comes from external data, not internal process

**Scenario 3: Executive decision-making**
- Human is expert, needs synthesis not process
- Trust comes from clarity, not methodology
- Outcome matters more than how you got there

### When Palette Agent System Wins

**Scenario 1: Iterative refinement**
- Multiple revision cycles expected
- Need to track what was tried and why
- Restartability matters (can resume later)

**Scenario 2: Agent maturity tracking**
- Building reusable agents for future projects
- Need to know which agents are reliable (WORKING/PRODUCTION)
- Impressions matter for long-term system improvement

**Scenario 3: Distributed team**
- Multiple people working on same project
- Need structured handoffs and decision lineage
- Glass-box architecture required (audit trail)

**Scenario 4: High-stakes ONE-WAY DOORS**
- Irreversible decisions require explicit confirmation
- Need to force human validation at critical points
- Accountability matters (who decided what, when)
